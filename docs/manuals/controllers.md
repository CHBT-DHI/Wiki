# Controllers

**Summary:** Adding, configuring, and testing feedback and feed-forward controllers in WEST.

**Prerequisites:** A model with at least one process unit that has a controllable input.

---

## What controllers do

Controllers adjust process inputs in response to measured signals — for example, modulating the blower output to maintain a dissolved oxygen setpoint.
